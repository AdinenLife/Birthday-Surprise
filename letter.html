My Feelings For You

Words from my heart to yours

April 20, 2025

My darling,

From the moment we first talked on Instagram, you've transformed my world in ways I never thought possible. Each day with you feels like a gift that I cherish more than anything else.

When I look at you, I see someone whose beauty transcends the physical - though you are truly stunning. Your kindness, your creativity, and your incredible strength inspire me daily. The way you laugh on my stupid jokes and childish behaviour, is the most beautiful sound I've ever heard. It makes my day.

You have been the best thing that has happened to me. Through every challenge we've faced, every triumph we've celebrated, my love for you has only grown stronger. You are one of the strongest and most beautiful person to exist in my life. I am very lucky to have you in my life.

What I treasure most are the quiet moments we share - watching movies together, talking late into the night, simply being in each other's presence. In those moments, I find a peace and happiness I never knew existed. You have been my light in my darkest days, my sun in the rainy sky. Thank you for everything that you do for me, my baby girl.

I want you to know that you deserve all the joy and love in the world. You make everything brighter just by being you, and I promise to spend every day trying to bring you the same happiness you bring to me and making myself better for you.

Thank you for being my partner, my supporter, and my best friend. I am so grateful that of all the people in this world, I get to call you mine.

Happy birthday, my love. Here's to many more years of adventures, laughter, and love.
